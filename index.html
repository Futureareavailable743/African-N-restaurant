Featured Dishes

Egusi Soup

Egusi soup is a rich and flavorful West African stew made with ground melon seeds, vegetables, meat or fish, and a blend of aromatic spices. The ground egusi seeds thicken the soup, giving it a unique texture, while the combination of ingredients creates a complex and satisfying taste. It's a hearty and delicious dish, often served with pounded yam, fufu, or rice. .

Chicken and jollof rice

Experience the vibrant flavors of West Africa with our Chicken and Jollof Rice. Tender, perfectly seasoned chicken is paired with a rich, flavorful Jollof rice, cooked in a tomato-based broth with aromatic spices. A classic combination that's sure to satisfy..

Barbecue

Indulge in the smoky goodness of our Barbecue. We slow-cook our meats to perfection, infusing them with a delicious smoky flavor. Whether you're craving ribs, chicken, or pulled pork, our barbecue is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser, served with your choice of sides.

Fried rice with chicken

Savor the delicious simplicity of our Fried Rice with Chicken. Fluffy rice is stir-fried with tender chicken pieces, fresh vegetables, and savory seasonings. A quick, satisfying, and flavorful meal that's perfect for any occasion.
